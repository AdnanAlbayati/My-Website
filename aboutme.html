About Me

Soon after, I started my own business, selling new, refurbished, and used laptops and PCs. I persisted until I became a well-known technology investor. If anyone is interested, I still sell perfect condition laptops and PCs with warranties. When the warranty expires, I continue to provide assistance and support, which my customers appreciate. I am also able to deliver online courses where I teach how to deal with electronic devices (laptops and PCs).
